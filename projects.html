COVID COLOR LAB.01 - TOKYO

In Covid Color Lab (CoLab), we attempt to envision new forms of interpersonal bonds and connections at a time of isolation and distancing. In this installation, we merged the colors chosen by the participants on an acrylic pour painting. In this immersive space, a visitor can reflect on their coexistence with others during this global crisis. While global pandemic has forever changed our expectations for public spaces and social gatherings, we hope to redeem our declining communal experience.
